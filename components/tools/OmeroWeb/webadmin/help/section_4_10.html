4.10. How to manage groups?

Click on the Groups button in the toolbar to display the list of existing groups (see Figure 4.10-1) You can then perform various Actions :

  • add new group (see Section 4.11),
  • delete group (Section 4.12)
  • edit groups (see Section 4.13),
  • edit users in the groups (see Section 4.14),
