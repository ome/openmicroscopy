4.11. How to add new group?

To add a new group:

  1. Click on the Groups button in the toolbar to display the list of existing groups (see Figure 4.9-1).
  2. Click on the Add new group button .
Then enter the following information:
  • The name of the group: field Group name .
  • The description of the group (optional): field Description .
  • The owner of the group: field Owner .
  • Select Save to confirm.
